alt_route 7-Step Text Preprocessing Pipeline

Step 1: Lowercase Conversion
Standardizes text format by casting all letters to lowercase.
Step 2: URL Removal
Cleans web link fragments using regular expression matching.
Step 3: HTML Tag Removal
Strips out any markup parameters (`<.*?>`) to isolate plain text content.
Step 4: Non-alphabetic Characters Filtering
Removes digits, special characters, and punctuation marks.
Step 5: Tokenization
Partitions full text strings into lists of individual word tokens.
Step 6: Stopword Removal
Filters out common semantic connectors (e.g., "the", "is", "at") using the NLTK English stopword list.
Step 7: Porter Stemming
Reduces tokens to their root stems (e.g., "running" and "runs" converge to "run").
